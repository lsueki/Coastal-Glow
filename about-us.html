Our Story

Coastal Glow is a female owned cosmetic company based in sunny Southern California. Founded by two best friends, Coastal Glow has grown to a team of around 100 dedicated employees since their initial launch in 2021. Our mission is to empower individuals to radiate their inner glow with high-quality products at an affordable price. The company specializes in sustainably produced, multi-use makeup products. Coastal Glow is committed to being environmentally responsible without compromising on performance.
